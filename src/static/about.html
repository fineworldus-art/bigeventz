About Big Eventz

Your Global Stage for Unforgettable Events

Our Story

Founded in 2020, Big Eventz emerged from a simple vision: to connect people worldwide through extraordinary events. What started as a small platform for local music festivals has grown into a global marketplace serving millions of event enthusiasts across six continents.

We believe that events have the power to bring people together, create lasting memories, and foster meaningful connections. Whether it's a music festival in London, a tech conference in Manchester, or a marathon in Edinburgh, every event tells a story and creates an experience worth sharing.
